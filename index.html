What is Webscraping? 


 Webscraping is the process of collecting and parsing (analyzing) large amounts of raw data from the Web.  

Applications of Webscraping

1. Yahoo Finance Stock Market Analysis:
Financial analysts use webscraping algorithms to predict trends based off of a company's historical stock price. This helps with maximizing return on investment.
